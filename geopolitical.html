By Mei ChenGeopolitical Desk, Senior CorrespondentSun, Jun 21

Beijing's destroyer encirclement of Taiwan is not a warning to Washington; it is the operational test of a quarantine architecture. The PLA Eastern Theater Command has been building this architecture since the August 2022 exercises that first closed the median line as deliberate policy rather than incident. The doctrine no longer requires a pretext.

Four hundred and thirty-five commissioned PLAN hulls give Beijing the rotation depth to sustain simultaneous presence across the Bashi Channel, the Miyako Strait, and Taiwan's eastern Pacific approaches without creating the observable resupply gaps that inform US carrier-group movement decisions. What the Wall Street Journal is reporting this week as a tightening is the addition of a fourth axis. The encirclement is now complete. That geometry places any inbound Seventh Fleet asset in a transit posture where its route is readable by PLA surface units before it arrives. The Lai Ching-te government's third-quarter 2026 defense appropriation review will produce the figure Beijing reads as the measure of how much Taipei believes deterrence still costs.

The appropriation-as-signal framing in the final sentence does more work than the geometry that precedes it. — WR
