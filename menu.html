Season 1 · The Family Classics | Season 2 · The Heart & Pressure Edit
A note on the kitchen. Every dish on this menu has been written for blood pressure mitigation and cholesterol balance. Dry rubs replace batter where possible. Soy is reduced or substituted with citrus and vinegar. Stocks are homemade. Coconut milk is halved. Fermented pastes -- miso, doenjang, gochujang -- are used at half quantity. Salt cod is desalted. Capers are rinsed.

On the numbers below. Nutrition figures are per single serving and assume the low-sodium method described. Cooked with full salt or bottled sauces, sodium can rise 3-5x. Calories and macros are reasonable approximations, not lab values.
