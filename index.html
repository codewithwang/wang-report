Charmaine Lo When Donald Trump called freeing Jimmy Lai a "tough one," the phrase arrived in Hong Kong carrying exactly the weight of what it did not say. This city has spent years learning to hold that kind of language precisely: the acknowledgment offered as courtesy, candour given in place of commitment. The record has it now; what comes next, the record does not say.-- CL
Maarten Bakker Washington is telling Taipei not to move for independence while declining to say whether it will defend the island if China moves anyway. That is not strategic ambiguity; it is the gradual withdrawal of a guarantee while preserving the right to claim credit for the relationship. Nine warships in the strait suggest at least one party read the signal correctly.-- MB
