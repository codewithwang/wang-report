Reporting tonight suggests Jimmy Lai's fate may turn less on what was argued in court than on whether Washington and Beijing find enough common ground to make him a useful piece to move. MB Three theaters of military signaling in one news cycle, Hormuz, the Taiwan Strait, and Pakistan, is less coincidence than structural symptom. Deterrence frameworks tend to fracture on multiple fronts at once because the calculations sustaining them were never truly independent of each other.
