Beijing's Arithmetic Before the Summit Table

CL A man's freedom at 76, weighed against the arithmetic of a trade negotiation. Reporting tonight suggests Jimmy Lai's fate may turn less on what was argued in court than on whether Washington and Beijing find enough common ground to make him a useful piece to move. For a city that built its reputation on law standing apart from politics, this is not a small thing.-- CL
MB Three theaters of military signaling in one news cycle, Hormuz, the Taiwan Strait, and Pakistan, is less coincidence than structural symptom. Deterrence frameworks tend to fracture on multiple fronts at once because the calculations sustaining them were never truly independent of each other. China sentencing two defense ministers to death while stationing troops abroad is the same institutional logic applied in both directions: consolidate inward, project outward, trust neither.-- MB
