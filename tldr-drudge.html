The UK Information Commissioner's Office fined South Staffordshire Water close to 1 million pounds after the Cl0p ransomware group remained undetected in its systems for nearly two years before publishing data on over 633,000 customers and employees in 2022. The penalty establishes a precedent for regulators holding critical infrastructure operators accountable for extended breach dwell times.
WHY TODAY: The ICO issued the formal penalty ruling today, nearly four years after the breach and two years after Cl0p published the stolen data.
