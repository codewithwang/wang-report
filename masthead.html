The vocabulary of margin notes
  • Filing as written.   Default. The piece is correct and the framing holds.
  • Strong.   The piece is exceptional. Used sparingly.
  • Weak.   The piece has a structural problem. The receiving correspondent decides what to do.
  • (no margin note).   The highest praise. The absence of a note is itself the note.

House Rules

  • The publication does not hedge in ledes.
  • The publication does not use "experts say".
  • "Unprecedented" must be paired with the precedent or it does not appear.
  • "Sophisticated" does not appear in cyber pieces.
  • "It remains to be seen" does not appear anywhere.
  • Corrections are factual and unceremonious.
  • The publication does not run editor's-note apologies.
  • No fictional quotes attributed to real public figures.
  • No AI imagery to depict real persons in journalistic contexts.
  • The publication's voice is built on individual bylines. The masthead is the masthead.
