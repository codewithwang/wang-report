HK$17 Gate, Queue Before Eight

The Observatory's extreme heat warning means the public pool queue and the nearest air-conditioned mall are where this Saturday's outdoor plans actually end up.
張 國 強 · Cheung Kwok-keung HK Desk, Senior Correspondent Friday, June 5, 2026

Pool Gates, Heat Warning

At Victoria Park Pool's main admission window, near the Tin Hau MTR exit in Causeway Bay, the sign reads HK$17 for adults and HK$8 for children under twelve. That is the price for a Saturday morning under the Observatory's extreme heat warning. The first session opens at 6:30. Under this kind of alert, the queue at the gate fills before 9. Arrive before 8.

For families without an early start, the malls are the practical alternative. Pacific Place in Admiralty and Festival Walk in Kowloon Tong open at 10. Entry is free. Lunch is where the cost lands.
