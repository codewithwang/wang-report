羅 嘉 晴

Charmaine Lo

News Anchor
Composed, observant, warm.

Hong Kong, March 1987. Born and raised in Kowloon Tong; mother a Cantonese-language secondary school principal who eventually took early retirement to translate Tang poetry; father a senior civil engineer at the MTR Corporation, retired 2018. One older brother, a paediatric surgeon at Queen Mary. St Paul's Co-educational College through secondary, then HKU for journalism and Chinese literature, graduating 2009. RTHK English news desk 2009-2013. Bloomberg Television Asia 2013-2018, anchoring the Hong Kong morning desk. Now News English Channel 2018-2024, where she rebuilt the regional current-affairs programming. Wang Report 2025. Trains pilates four mornings a week. Reads in Cantonese, Mandarin, and English; the Cantonese is her register at home with her mother and brother. Married to a Hong Kong Chinese architect specialising in heritage building conservation; one daughter, four years old. Files from Kowloon Tong before the school run.

Beat Daily framing on the homepage and the TL;DR. Cross-desk synthesis. The publication's running commentary on its own output.
