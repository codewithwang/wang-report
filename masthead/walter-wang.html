He built this publication and edits it. He does not appear in person on the page; the byline is always WR.

The Vocabulary of Margin Notes

  • Filing as writtenDefault. The piece is correct and the framing holds. Roughly 70% of pieces.
  • StrongThe piece is exceptional. Used sparingly. Roughly 10%.
  • WeakThe piece has a structural problem. The receiving correspondent decides what to do. 5–10%.
  • (no margin note)The highest praise. The absence of a note is itself the note. Roughly 10%.
