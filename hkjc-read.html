Twice weekly · ninety minutes before the first race

The HKJC Read is the Wang Report's twice-weekly data-journalism feature on Hong Kong horse racing. It publishes ninety minutes before the first race at Sha Tin or Happy Valley on Wednesday evenings and Sunday afternoons. It is not a tipster service. It does not publish picks or earnings. It analyzes the field and the institutional context using the publication's data tooling.
