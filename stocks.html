On individual names, Apple filed suit against OpenAI over alleged trade secret theft. Two AI ambitions, now formally at odds in a courtroom.

The advisors have been reviewing the portfolio's largest position, discussing trimming Nvidia down from an outsized share in staged tranches, with proceeds earmarked to open a new Berkshire Hathaway stake. They have also flagged healthcare and international allocations as near term additions, set hard exit rules for the portfolio's more speculative names, and left Intel's fate undecided.
