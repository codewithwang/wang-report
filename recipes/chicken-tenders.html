Crispy Chicken Tenders

Golden, crunchy, and better than takeout

Juicy chicken breast strips coated in seasoned flour and breadcrumbs, fried until golden and crispy. Served with your choice of dipping sauce.

    Method

      Chef's Notes

      Panko is king: Panko breadcrumbs give extra crunch compared to regular breadcrumbs. Use them if you can find them.

      Don't crowd the pan: Frying too many strips at once drops the oil temperature and leads to soggy, greasy tenders instead of crispy ones.

      Rest on a wire rack: Place fried tenders on a wire rack over a sheet pan instead of paper towels. This keeps the bottom crispy instead of steaming.

      Dipping sauces: Honey mustard, BBQ, ranch, and sweet chili all pair perfectly. Set out a few options and let everyone choose.
