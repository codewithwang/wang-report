Sunday, May 10, 2026
CL Jimmy Lai's fate is now part of a summit negotiation this city did not initiate and cannot observe. If Trump and Xi sit down this week, people in Hong Kong know his name will enter the room in some form, attached to no official agenda, subject to no local input. That is what it feels like when consequential things happen to your city rather than with it.-- CL
MB Sanctions on Hong Kong entities for Iran arms supply land two days before the Trump-Xi summit, and the Hormuz closure arrives in the same cycle, which suggests the Iran file is functioning less as a crisis than as a variable in a negotiation. North Korea, separately, has constitutionally automated its nuclear response to decapitation, shifting deterrence from judgment to procedure. Institutions are designing themselves to act without deliberating.-- MB
CLPyongyang has written automatic nuclear retaliation into its constitution, which means its arsenal no longer requires a living leader to decide when to fire.
